Design on demand

Both the interior and exterior design can be customized according to your preferences. Choose between six exclusive exterior colours – all available in metallic or matte versions – carefully selected to match the design of the car. Add a unique touch with your personal choice of upholsteries, rims and brightwork, making sure you get the Polestar 1 the way you want it.

Panoramic experience

The Polestar 1 comes with a fixed panoramic glass roof that stretches the length and width of the car. It`s been integrated seamlessly offering an unobstructed overhead view. Integrated into the roof is a leather-clad console which provides control over certain functions in the car. But the most unique aspect of the panel is the illuminated Polestar symbol reflected in the glass above, like a star in the sky.

Performance and convenience

Polestar 1 offers unparelleled performance and seamless integration into your daily life. From the driver's seat, you control a unique and intuitive HMI with a suite of navigation, communication, and entertainment systems.

Specifications

  • Engine type: 2-liter In-line 4-cyl. supercharged and turbocharged
  • Electric motors: 2 rear axle electric machines + Crank ISG
  • Power: 600 hp
  • Torque: 1000 Nm
  • Electric range: 150 km (93 miles) (est. in pure mode)
  • Batteries: 34kWh arranged in 3 battery stacks (2 packs)
  • Suspension: Öhlins® + double wishbone (front) / integral link (rear)
  • Brakes: Akebono 6-piston calipers (front) / Conti EPB (rear)
  • Body type: CFRP + steel compound body
  • Tire size: 275/30 R21 (Front) / 295/30 R21 (Rear)
